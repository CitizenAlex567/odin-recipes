Chicken curry recipe

A soft, moist, and simple homemade cake using yogurt as the main measure—perfect for beginners.

  • 1 natural yogurt (125 g)
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 yogurt cups of flour3 yogurt cups of flour
  • 2 yogurt cups of sugar
  • 1 yogurt cup of oil
  • 1 packet baking powder
  • Lemon zest (optional)
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Whisk the eggs and sugar until foamy.
  3. Add the yogurt and oil, mix well.
  4. Add the flour and baking powder (sifted). Mix until smooth.
  5. Add lemon zest if using.
  6. Pour into a greased mold and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  7. Let cool before removing from the mold.
