Classic Yogurt Sponge Cake

A creamy, flavorful chicken curry made with coconut milk and mild spices. Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

  • 500 g chicken breast, cubed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 200 ml coconut milk
  • 1 tsp turmeric (optional)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  1. Heat the oil in a large pan and sauté the onion until soft.
  2. Add the garlic and cook for 1 more minute.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Stir in the curry powder and turmeric.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and simmer for 10–15 minutes until thickened.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, and serve with white rice.Season with salt and pepper, and serve with white rice.
